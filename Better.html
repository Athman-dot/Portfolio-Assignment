Better School Choice

An application that enables parents to easily search for nearby schools.

Problem

Parents often struggle to discover and compare nearby schools efficiently. This project simplifies school search by focusing on accessibility, location relevance, and clarity of information.

Research Insights

Parents emphasized the need for quick comparisons and trusted reviews when choosing schools. Research showed that location, affordability, and credibility were the top decision factors.

Design Process

We analyzed parent pain points, created user flows, and iterated through wireframes to make school discovery intuitive, visual, and quick to act on.

User Flow

Mapped how users move from entering a location to viewing school details, ensuring minimal steps and clear next actions throughout the journey.

Low-Fidelity Wireframes

Outlined the basic structure and layout, focusing on simplifying the search input and displaying results in an easy-to-scan format.

High-Fidelity Prototype

Applied final visuals, typography, and icons to deliver a polished experience emphasizing simplicity, speed, and reliability in school discovery.

Outcome

Parents can now find schools nearby quickly, compare essential details, and make informed decisions through a seamless, map-based interface.
