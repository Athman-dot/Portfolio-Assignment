Jam-Bi: YouTube Clone

An improved version of YouTube with enhanced community and discovery features.

Problem

Current video platforms suffer from cluttered navigation, fragmented content experiences, and poor discovery, making it harder for users to engage and creators to reach their audiences effectively.

Research Insights

User interviews revealed that creators want simpler, more intuitive navigation and a unified experience across content types. Insights highlighted the need for better search accuracy and smoother transitions between video formats.

Design Process

We mapped user flows, built wireframes, and tested prototypes to simplify navigation and create a more unified viewing experience.

User Flow

Visualized the key user paths to simplify navigation and reduce friction between actions. These flows helped ensure a more intuitive experience when exploring content and switching between Shorts, subscriptions, and search results.

Outcome
